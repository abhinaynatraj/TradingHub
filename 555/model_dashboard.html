Setup Conditions
1
SMT Divergence
NQ sweeps a 5-minute swing high or low while ES does NOT sweep its corresponding swing level — indicating smart money divergence between the two instruments.
2
Inverse FVG (IFVG)
A 3-candle fair value gap that gets inverted (price trades through it). Checked on both 3-minute and 5-minute timeframes. Minimum gap size: 2 points.
3
CISD Confirmation
Change in State of Delivery: body-only close beyond the delivery threshold, confirming directional commitment. Both IFVG and CISD must occur (in any order) after the SMT divergence.
