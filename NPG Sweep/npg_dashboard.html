NPG Sweep · Probability Engine

What this measures
Wick Lick → CISD → projection statistics across 3 HTF/LTF pairings
Implements the npg "Sweep · CISD · FVG · Key Levels" model (the Fearing methodology). A Wick Lick is a higher-timeframe candle that pierces the prior candle's high or low and closes back inside it — liquidity grab + rejection. Once a Wick Lick prints, the engine walks the lower timeframe for a CISD (Change in State of Delivery): a closing break of the opposing-candle series that built up to the sweep. Entry fires on the next bar; targets are 0.5×, 1.0×, 1.5×, 2.0× of the opposing series' range; stop is the sweep extreme. Each trade splits into 4 partial-exit legs (25% per target).
Pairings: 1H_5M · 4H_15M · D_1H
Profiles: series_multi (4 partial exits) · raw_measure (no SL/TP, MAE/MFE only)
Filters: Silver (late-week + aggressive close) · SMT (NQ-ES divergence) · Direction · Session
