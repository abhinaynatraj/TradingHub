Breakout Trade Model

From analysis to a tradeable model
What does the breakout edge look like with realistic stops, sizing, and slippage rules?
The headline analysis says the next hour follows through ~70% of the time. That's a wick statistic — not a trade. This page resolves every breakout into an actual trade under three different stop-placement rules, sized for MNQ ($2/pt, $225 risk per trade), with same-bar tie-breaks resolving to the stop. Most setups get rejected by the risk gate — that's the honest tradeoff between structural stops and capital efficiency.
